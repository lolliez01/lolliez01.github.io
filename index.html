About Us

COAST 2 COAST PRECISION ENGINEERING AND FABRICATION is a leading global-minded provider of specialized industrial engineering services, proudly operating from our base in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. We provide comprehensive solutions encompassing heavy fabrication, advanced welding, intricate equipment manufacturing, and the full lifecycle management of industrial process systems and infrastructure. With a steadfast commitment to precision, durability, and a robust safety culture, we deliver innovative and compliant solutions that meet the demanding requirements of diverse heavy industries worldwide, ensuring unparalleled operational efficiency, safety, and longevity.
