About Us

COAST 2 COAST PRECISION ENGINEERING AND FABRICATION is a leading global-minded provider of specialized industrial engineering services, proudly operating from our base in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. We provide comprehensive solutions encompassing heavy fabrication, advanced welding, intricate equipment manufacturing, and the full lifecycle management of industrial process systems and infrastructure. With a steadfast commitment to precision, durability, and a robust safety culture, we deliver innovative and compliant solutions that meet the demanding requirements of diverse heavy industries worldwide, ensuring unparalleled operational efficiency, safety, and longevity.

Our Specialities & Services

Delivering precision-engineered solutions for heavy industry.

Advanced Fabrication

Fabrication of Pressure Vessels, Storage Tanks, and Process Components for high-pressure or large-volume containment.

Heat Exchanger & Coil Services

Design, fabrication, repair, and assembly of heat exchange equipment, including shell-and-tube exchangers and condensers.

Large-Scale Rotating Equipment

Specialized rigging, alignment, and maintenance of large equipment like kilns, dryers, and filter/mould drums.

Industrial Piping Systems

End-to-end solutions for industrial piping, including fabrication, installation, repair, and Non-Destructive Testing (NDT).

Heavy Lifting & Rigging

Managing heavy lifting operations, meticulous lift planning, industrial demolition, and complex site logistics.

Precision Welding & NDT

High-standard welding by certified welders, verified with NDT methods like DPT and MPI for guaranteed quality.
